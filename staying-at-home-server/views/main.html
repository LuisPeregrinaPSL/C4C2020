Staying@Home for a cause

Gamifying and analyzing People at Home data to improve efficiency of public actions to reduce virus spread during health emergencies

Why contribute?

The most effective action to prevent virus spread is the lockdown and/or stay at home policies.

Countries are facing big challenges to motivate people to stay at home.

Multiple measures being implemented but resources are never enough to cover broad areas.

Together we can help our public health systems and government to optimize resources available.
