Get Fun while Staying at Home for a cause

Track your time at home with our VR Mobile platform, share your achievements and motivate others to stay at home

Mobile App

Track your time at home, challenge others and share your achievements on social networks. Built with Ionic Framework.

Virtual Reality Forest

Build your forest and get a virtual experience while navigating around the environment. Built with A-Frame.

Public Dashboard

Access public reports about people staying at home on different coutries, states and cities. Built with Kibana and Elastic Search.

Geolocation

Using GPS location the platform tracks how log the user stays at home. Built with Ionic Capacitor.
